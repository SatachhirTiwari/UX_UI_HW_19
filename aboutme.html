About Me

My life has been deeply shaped by the numerous multicultural experiences. I am a joyful, fun loving, positive and easy going person.

My passion for UX revolves around the joy of exploring users’ unmet needs and enabling them to solve it in the easiest possible way. Keen curiosity and sincere empathy continues to empower my method of working while avoiding users perspective blindspots.

I was born and brought up in India in a traditional family with deep family roots. I completed my bachelors in BioMedical Engineering at Lucknow, India. I moved to USA after my marriage and currently live in Virginia. I like spending time with my family and friends. Being a creative person, I love to paint, do check out some of my art work here.
